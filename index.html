Video Games

In regards to my favorite game, my number one is split between two games. Minecraft and Rimworld!

A personal screenshot from a modded Minecraft server that I hosted

In Minecraft, a thing that I often enjoy finding myself doing is building contraptions of automation both in base game as well as in modded play. Much of said modded play consists of playing on expansive technology related modpacks that ill get lost in for long periods of time

For Rimworld a thing that I enjoy about the game is playing out scenarios. Be it operating a containment facility of scientists researching and delving into the nature of lovecraftian horrors or running a gang of bandits who are out to corner the organ market through the use of a variety of war crimes! In addition to all this, the rather extensive modding platform that the game has which further expands the possibilities within this game

A video of my Anomaly Rimworld Colony

Here are some various other games that I enjoy in no particular order!

  • Squad - A massively multiplayer tactical first person shooter that bridges the gap between arcade shooters and military simulators with realistic combat and logistics, along with simplification of typical milsim features
  • Dwarf Fortress - The grandfather of colony simulators about managing a fort of dwarves with a intricate random generator capable of producing complex lore and worlds. An inspiration to many sandbox and colony sims, like Minecraft and Rimworld
  • Rainbow Six Siege - A competitive tactical first person shooter about swatting scenarios in urban areas with close quarters combat and destructible environments
  • Team Fortress 2 - A class and team based arena shooter that pioneered their genres with a wacky cast of characters and unique style. Backed by a bolstering community constantly creating fan content
  • Lethal Company - A horror game about exploring moons and collecting scrap from abandoned facilities for your company. Work to hit quota before getting gear to make your life a little bit less painful
  • Space Station 13 and 14 - A role playing game injected into a atmospherics simulator! play among others to run a slowly failing space station plagued with traitors, aliens, wizards, dragons, and more
  • Foxhole - A massively multiplayer military simulator based in the world wars. Work together with your faction to run logistics or military operations against the opposing faction in multi-week long wars
  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild - An action adventure game about saving a kingdom from a great evil. Play as the legendary warrior Link to save the Kingdom of Hyrule by defeating Calamity Ganon

About

An old screenshot of the website before the revamp circa 2024

Originally hosted on Replit, this was just a little website that I created as a small gag a few years back to share rick rolls and funny videos under a personal website. I often would forget of the existence of the site, only occasionally getting DMs about being rick rolled because someone had found a ricked link to my site. However, on November 21st of 2024, I would become reinvigorated into working on my site after being made aware of a recently growing "Indie Web". To keep it short, it is basically a little movement to go against the corporate web and to relive old times where the world wide web was much more independent and unique. The popular sites of today are so corporatized and all similar looking. Think Twitter, Reddit, and Youtube versus those retro style websites, blogs, and forums from the early days of the internet
